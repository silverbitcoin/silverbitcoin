# SilverBitcoin Tokenomics

## Overview

SilverBitcoin implements a carefully designed tokenomics model with a **hard cap of 1 billion SBTC** that ensures long-term sustainability and deflationary mechanics.

## Total Supply

- **Maximum Supply**: 1,000,000,000 SBTC (1 Billion - HARD CAP)
- **Decimals**: 9 (1 SBTC = 1,000,000,000 MIST)
- **Genesis Allocation**: All 1B minted at genesis
- **Emission**: 20-year schedule from Validator Rewards Pool
- **Fee Burning**: 30% → 80% (increasing over time)
- **Long-term**: Deflationary from Year 11 onwards

## Fee Burning Mechanism

### How It Works
1. **Transaction Fees**: Users pay fees in SBTC for transactions
2. **Burning Percentage**: A percentage of fees are burned (removed from circulation)
3. **Increasing Over Time**: Burning percentage increases from 30% to 80%
4. **Deflationary Effect**: Creates scarcity and upward price pressure

### Burning Schedule
- **Years 1-5**: 30% of fees burned
- **Years 6-10**: 50% of fees burned
- **Years 11-20**: 70% of fees burned
- **Year 20+**: 80% of fees burned

### Example
If 1M SBTC in fees are collected in Year 1:
- Burned: 300K SBTC (30%)
- Validator Rewards: 700K SBTC (70%)

If 1M SBTC in fees are collected in Year 20+:
- Burned: 800K SBTC (80%)
- Validator Rewards: 200K SBTC (20%)
