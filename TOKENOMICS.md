## 🔐 Full DPOS Validator Requirements

### Minimum Stake: 100 SBTC per Validator

**Rationale:**
- Prevents spam and sybil attacks
- Ensures validator commitment and skin-in-the-game
- Aligns with industry standards (Cosmos, Avalanche)
- Limits validator set to ~10 Million maximum (1B / 100)

### Validator Economics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Minimum Stake** | 100 SBTC |
| **Max Validators** | ~10,000,000 |
| **Slashing Penalty** | 5-10% for misbehavior |
| **Unbonding Period** | 21 days |
| **Commission Rate** | 0-20% (validator configurable) |
| **APY** | 20-30%+ (decreases as supply grows) |

### Validator Rewards

**Phase 1 (Year 1-5):** 50M SBTC/year
- Emission: 40M SBTC/year (80%)
- Fuel Fees: 10M SBTC/year (20%)

**Phase 2 (Year 6-10):** 30M SBTC/year
- Emission: 18M SBTC/year (60%)
- Fuel Fees: 30M SBTC/year (40%)

**Phase 3 (Year 11-20):** 10M SBTC/year
- Emission: 3M SBTC/year (30%)
- Fuel Fees: 100M SBTC/year (70%)

**Phase 4 (Year 20+):** 0 Emission
- Fuel Fees: 150M+ SBTC/year (100%)

### Validator Slashing

- **Double Signing:** 10% slash
- **Downtime:** 0.01% per block (max 5%)
- **Invalid Blocks:** 5% slash
- **Jailing Period:** 10 blocks minimum

### Delegation

- Delegators can stake any amount (minimum 1 SBTC)
- Validators earn commission on delegated stake
- Rewards distributed proportionally
- Unbonding: 21 days lockup period
